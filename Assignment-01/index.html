About The Expo

Innovation • Robotics • IoT • Artificial Intelligence

The UET IoT & Robotics Innovation Expo 2026 is a technology event organized to showcase innovative projects developed by university students. The expo includes robotics competitions, IoT exhibitions, AI demonstrations, embedded systems workshops and networking opportunities with faculty members and industry professionals.
